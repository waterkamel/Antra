About Antra Shoe Store

Antra Shoe Store founded in early 2020 by Antonny Muraya and located in the city of Nairobi, Kenya. Antra Shoe Store works closely with well-known and local brands, therefore the products sold by Antra Shoe Store are original brands that have partnered together.

The aims of the Antra Shoe Store is that people can get authentic goods from well-known and local brands at standard prices and help introduce local brands so that local brands can grow and participate in advancing the fashion industry in Kenya.
